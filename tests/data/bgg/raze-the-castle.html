Description Edit | History

Free print-and-play game about expanding your kingdom and protecting it from opponents' attackers and special invaders.

Each turn resolve events, pay upkeep, build structures and hire defenders. Play until one player's castle has been destroyed in combat, then largest and strongest kingdom wins.
